About this book

Outliers is mostly about success. If you want to do well later on, then this book is for you.

Outliers is the best book if you want to know how to succeed. For example, Bill Gates, the Beatles, Bill Joy, and why hockey players aren't born in the fall. Why is the question it answers. When we dive deeper, there's a lot of factors to success.

Outliers is an entertaining book of life and success. Everything you need to know about successful is in this book. This book is 280 pages (Not Including notes) pretty long it has a few pictures along the way. (At most half a page each picture)
